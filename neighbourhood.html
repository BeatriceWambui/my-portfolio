Doula care is a project built for pre and post natal mothers.This project helps this mothers to know how to live healthy during and after pregnancy. The project, has a BMI calculator where mothers can check their BMI they are in status, and know how they are fairing. Doula care also has a Trimester tracker based on your entry of the pregnancy month. For fathers or husbands with this pregnant mothers, they are also considered in this project by a feature where you specify your gender and if male, the project then gives you advice on how to support your wife on this journey to delivery and after delivery.
